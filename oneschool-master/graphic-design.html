Graphic Design

Graphics Design

Master Visual Communication

The CodeBayTech Graphic Design course provides a thorough education in visual design, equipping you with the skills to create compelling graphics, brand identities, and digital media that communicate effectively and aesthetically.

Core Learning Areas

  • Design Principles: Master color theory, typography, and layout fundamentals.
  • Digital Tools: Gain proficiency in industry-standard software such as Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.
  • Branding: Learn to create cohesive brand identities and visual assets.
  • Web & Mobile Design: Design user-friendly interfaces for web and mobile platforms.
  • Portfolio Development: Build a professional portfolio showcasing your creative projects.

Who should Enroll

  • Aspiring Graphic Designers: Start your career with a strong foundation in design principles and tools.
  • Marketing Professionals: Enhance your visual communication skills to improve brand messaging.
  • Creatives: Expand your abilities in digital design and media creation.

Program Outcomes

  • Proficiency in key graphic design software and techniques.
  • Experience in developing cohesive brand identities and digital media.
  • A polished portfolio that demonstrates your design capabilities.
